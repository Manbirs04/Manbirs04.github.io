Space Exploration Encyclopedia

Mars Exploration

Current Mars exploration missions include the Mars rovers like Curiosity and Perseverance. These missions aim to study the Martian surface, geology, and search for signs of past or present life.

International Space Station

The International Space Station (ISS) serves as a hub for scientific research in space. It's a joint project involving multiple countries, and astronauts on the ISS conduct experiments in various fields, including biology, physics, and astronomy.

Upcoming Missions

Several exciting space missions are on the horizon, including plans for human missions to Mars, the exploration of distant asteroids, and the search for exoplanets that may harbor life. The future of space exploration looks promising.
